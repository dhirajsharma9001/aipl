About

Aipl Business Club

Engineered to perfection, AIPL Business Club is a futuristic office project and will be the latest addition in AIPL’s diversified portfolio of Real Estate Offerings. Spread over 4.18 acres in Sector 62, Gurgaon on Golf Course Extension Road. The commercial Space offers 663000 Sq. ft. of office with floor plates ranging from 12000 sq. ft. to 51000 sq. ft.

AIPL Business Club has been designed for the new needs of the workforce with the greatest possible comfort in a modern, pleasant work environment in order to optimally meet the business objectives of the occupier.

The principal Architects of the project, Morphogenesis are one of leading architects in the world and the recipients of numerous Nationals and International awards. AIPL Business Club is a novel concept that creates the perfect blend of business and pleasure. Aptly called ‘Business at Leisure’. A perfect business destination.
