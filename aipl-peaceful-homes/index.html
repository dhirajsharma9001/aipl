Zen Residences

Zen Residences is a thoughtfully designed gated development that is set back from the street, giving you a feeling of tranquility and bliss. Comprising of 2 and 3 BHK units in sizes 1262sq.ft & 1655 sq. ft., respectively, Zen Residences benefit from a prime location which offers an endearing view of the Aravalli range. These flats in Gurgaon are a part of 11.5 acres group housing complex. They are characterized by an eco-friendly design and sustainable features with mini club & lounge facilities in the still area under each tower. Access to shopping center and club house, these 2 BHK & 3 BHK flats in Gurgaon offer premium amenities. The complex also offers 24 hrs security, 24*7 water supply, power back up, fire protection systems, landscaped gardens, sports facilities making it an excellent option for families looking for an apartment for sale in Gurgaon.

Club Residences

Walk into a setting quite like this: a quiet, purely residential community with lush natural landscapes, life-giving water all around, and breathtaking view of the Aravallis. With nothing separating you from nature, you can retreat into your peaceful surroundings or venture a few steps beyond and engage with the excitement around you. Apartments at AIPL Club Residences on Golf Course Extension Road, Gurgaon are premium compact apartments with a contemporary architecture, an extensive range of luxury amenities and the panoramic view of Aravallis. These 2 BHK flats in Gurgaon are an exclusive choice for young couples and nuclear families.
